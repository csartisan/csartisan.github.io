Creative Writing sophomore Ruth Klinman is organizing a Lunar New Year Zine to celebrate Asian culture! Prose, poetry, visual arts– including digital and traditional art as well as photography– are all accepted. Submissions must be Lunar New Year themed, but this can have a variety of different meanings, so be creative! This could mean the celebration itself, traditions involving Lunar New Year, the history and legends behind it, the meaning of Chinese zodiacs, and so much more! Submit your work with the QR code below before submissions close April 25th.
