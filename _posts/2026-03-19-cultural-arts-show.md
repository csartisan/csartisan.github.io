Looking for an opportunity to share your culture through art? The Cultural Arts Club is hosting CSArts-SGV’s first student-led Cultural Arts Show. 

The Cultural Arts Show is an art exhibition celebrating the heritage and creativity of our school. With so many diverse perspectives, it serves as a space for students to fully express themselves and feel seen and heard.

“The world is going through many changes, so coming together is very important. I love seeing people engage with each other and making an impact through art,” said freshman Sienna Lucio.

All forms of artwork are accepted as long as they conform to the theme of cultural expression. This includes poetry, painting, sculpture, and live acts. The completed gallery will be on display during the 2026-2027 school year.

Submissions are open now through April 17, 2026, and all students, regardless of grade level or conservatory, are encouraged to participate.  For additional questions, please contact [Sienna.Lucio@sgv.csarts.net](mailto:Sienna.Lucio@sgv.csarts.net) or [Grace.Harbicht@sgv.csarts.net](mailto:Grace.Harbicht@sgv.csarts.net).
